Biography

Cristiano Ronaldo was born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal. From a young age, he showed incredible talent in football. He joined Sporting Lisbon's youth academy and quickly caught the attention of top European clubs.

He began his professional career at Sporting Lisbon before moving to Manchester United in 2003, where he won multiple Premier League titles and his first Ballon d'Or.

After Manchester United, Ronaldo played for Real Madrid, Juventus, and returned to Manchester United, breaking record after record along the way.
