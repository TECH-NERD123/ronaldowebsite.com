Achievements of Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the most decorated footballers in history. He has consistently broken records and inspired millions worldwide with his dedication and skill.

Year Achievement
2008 Ballon d'Or Winner
2013 UEFA Best Player in Europe
2016 European Championship Winner with Portugal
2017 UEFA Champions League Winner
2022 All-time top scorer for Real Madrid

Career Timeline

  • 2003 - Joins Sporting Lisbon
  • 2003-2009 - Manchester United
  • 2009-2018 - Real Madrid
  • 2018-2022 - Juventus
  • 2022-Present - Returns to Manchester United & Currently Plays for Al Nassr

These achievements highlight Ronaldo's incredible consistency, determination, and ability to perform at the highest level year after year.
